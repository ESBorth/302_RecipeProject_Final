Borth's Best: Chicken Pot Pie Stew

A hearty meal perfect for cold weather or sick-days.
This recipe is a sure-fire way to raise spirits!

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 2 hours | Servings: 10


Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cooked chicken breast, cubed
  • 32 oz frozen mixed veggies
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2-3 large potatoes, boiled and cubed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 4 cups chicken broth


Instructions

  1. Make sure chicken and potatoes are fully cooked and diced.
  2. Rinse off veggies and raise to room temperature.
  3. Melt butter over medium heat.
  4. Once melted, add in flour, salt, and pepper. Continue to stir until mixture is firm, but bubbling.
  5. Remove from heat and slowly add broth and milk. Boil for two minutes or mixture thickens.
  6. Lower temperature once thickened, and add veggies, potato, and chicken.
  7. Allow to simmer for 30 minutes, and allow another 20 to cool before serving.
